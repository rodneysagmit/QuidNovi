Loan Terms at Quid Novi Acquisitions LLC

Welcome to the Loan Terms page of Quid Novi Acquisitions LLC. At QNA, we maintain a robust financing strategy that allows us to leverage opportunities in the public equities market while ensuring sustainable growth. Here, we outline the favorable loan conditions we offer, structured to meet our financing needs through strategic partnerships with banks, credit unions, insurance companies, pension funds, and other private entities.

Our Loan Offerings

QNA is dedicated to fostering growth and ensuring the success of our operations and, by extension, our partners. We offer competitive loan terms, detailed as follows:

One-Year Loans
  • Annual Interest Rate: 12%
  • Coupon Payments: Quarterly, providing regular returns on the lent capital throughout the loan term.
  • Term: 12 months, designed for entities looking for short-term investment opportunities with reliable returns.
Two-Year Loans
  • Annual Interest Rate: 20%
  • Coupon Payments: Zero, as the interest accrues and is paid in full at the end of the loan term, maximizing the potential return on investment.
  • Term: 24 months, suited for partners seeking higher returns over a longer investment period.
Why Lend to QNA?
  • Secured Investments: All loans and interests are secured against our substantial portfolio of publicly-traded shares and equities, ensuring the safety of your investment.
  • Competitive Returns: Our loan terms are designed to offer attractive returns to our lenders, reflecting the confidence we have in our business model and the security we provide.
  • Strategic Partnership: Lending to QNA is not just about the attractive returns; it's about becoming a part of a strategic partnership that values growth, sustainability, and mutual success.
  • Transparency and Integrity: We operate with the highest levels of transparency and integrity, ensuring our partners are well-informed and confident in their decision to support our operations.
