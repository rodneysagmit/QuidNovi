About

Quid Novi Acquisitions LLC (QNA) is a private enterprise, legally-registered in State of California. QNA engages in business of acquiring publicly-traded shares and equities. By successfully employing carefully-structured strategies/solutions, QNA is able to generate impressive-level profits for our share-holding companies.

QNA is not a private equity company, nor an investment vehicle for investing public. We do not seek investment capital or investors.

Whenever QNA needs fresh capital, we borrow short/long term loans from banks, credit unions, insurance companies, pension funds, and other private entities. We pledge all our capital, holdings of publicly-traded shares and equities, to guarantee repayment of all loans and interest we have taken out.
